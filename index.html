Car accidents across the U.S. have remained a major problem over the years.

Have you wondered WHAT is leading to the high number of crashes across the states?

Let's take a look at the Types of Vehicles that contribute most to these fatal crashes.


  • The sankey graph explains the percentages of categories and sub categories of vehicle types that contributed to the car crashes from 2016-2019.
  • It is found Passenger cars contribute the most to the total number of accidents followed by light trucks.
  • Upon deep dive into subcategory, it is seen that 4-Door Sedan (26%) and Light Pickup trucks (17%) contribute significantly to the crashes.

So how do you think weather affects car accidents? Let's see if there's any correlation.

Weather Conditions at the Time of Accidents

  • The graph on this page shows the Weather distribution across various states at the time of accident in the USA.

  • It is observed that most of the accidents occur in clear conditions.

  • This can be attributed to the idea that clear weather is more common throughout the year in general and traffic is also heavier on clear days.

But which months of the year have the highest accident rates?

Fatal Crashes By Month

  • From the line charts, it's visible that frequency of crashes do have a tendency to go up in the later months of the year in the United States.

  • Specifically, the months of October, 2016 and July,2017 saw the highest spike in accident numbers.

How does Alcohol affect the rate of accidents in the United States?

  • Alcohol is responsible for roughly 32% of all accidents.
  • As per the statistics, alcohol remains the largest deterministic factor behind the car accidents in United States.
  • The bar chart above provides us with the top 10 states with the most drunk driving accidents.
  • Wyoming, North Dakota, Montana and Idaho have the highest percentage of alcohol related car crashes in their states.

Key Findings

  • The biggest factor behind car accidents has been alcohol.
  • 8-9AM and 6-7 PM seem to be the peak hour for accidents.
  • Most accidents seem to occur in clear weather .
  • 4 door sedans are the vehicle type that seem to be involved in most accidents.
